# Spy Registry

The master record of all double-agent NPCs. One entry per spy. Each entry contains the spy's cover identity, true loyalty, the faction they have infiltrated, the burn condition that exposes them, and the player Perception rank required to detect them before the burn condition fires.

This document is the in-world equivalent of what Auditor Vesh of the Gray Compact would be carrying if he defects. If Vesh defects and the player receives this document in-game, they receive a filtered version — not every entry, only the ones Vesh has confirmed.

---

## Active spy operations

### 1 — Gray Compact agent in the Aureate Covenant
**NPC name:** Nelle Davar
**Cover identity:** Junior records clerk. Four years in post. Unremarkable record. No disciplinary flags.
**True loyalty:** The Gray Compact
**Handler:** Factor Renne Saul
**Infiltrated faction:** The Aureate Covenant (administrative layer)
**Location:** Sanctum Ward — Office of the Record
**Mission:** Monitor Triune internal communications; track the deadlock; report when it breaks and which direction it breaks toward.
**Burn condition:** The Triune deadlock breaks and the new majority calls a full loyalty audit of all administrative staff. Nelle's Compact-sourced financial records surface during the audit.
**Player detection:** Perception rank 9+ during any administrative quest inside the Sanctum. Passive observation reveals expense patterns inconsistent with a junior clerk's salary.
**NPC tier:** 4
**Status:** Active

### 2 — Aureate Covenant agent in the Ashen Veil
**NPC name:** *(unnamed — identity withheld even from this registry; known only to Leth)*
**Cover identity:** Necromantic scholar who defected from a smaller dark cult three years ago. Mid-rank. Clean record. Has contributed legitimately to two published Veil research papers.
**True loyalty:** Aureate Covenant Inquisitorial Sub-Order
**Handler:** Inquisitor-Commander Leth
**Infiltrated faction:** The Ashen Veil (scholarly tier)
**Location:** Ashgate Quarter — embedded in Veil scholarly operations
**Mission:** Document Veil ritual methodology for counter-measure development. Specifically: map the threshold experiment's progress.
**Burn condition:** Scholar-Physician Dael begins asking questions about the energy source. The Covenant agent provides a redirect that is too smooth — protective rather than genuinely curious. Dael notices. Reports it.
**Player detection:** Perception rank 8+ during a Veil scholarly scene. The agent's redirect pattern reads as deflection when Dael asks about energy sourcing.
**NPC tier:** 4
**Status:** Active — approaching burn risk as Dael's disillusionment score rises

### 3 — Ashen Veil agent in the Verdant Circle
**NPC name:** *(unnamed — Ironthorn-external; not yet in roster)*
**Cover identity:** Ranger-initiate. Joined three years ago. Clean performance record. Has participated in two Severance operations.
**True loyalty:** The Ashen Veil
**Handler:** Arch-Necromancer Vorath
**Infiltrated faction:** The Verdant Circle (outer ring)
**Mission:** Study Circle ritual magic — specifically the Root Court's connection to the forest consciousness — for potential threshold applications.
**Burn condition:** Warden Coln performs a Severance in the region where the spy claimed to have been raised. The spy's emotional response is inconsistent with grief — it is fascination. Coln registers it.
**Player detection:** Perception rank 7+ in any scene involving the spy during or after a Severance. Physical tell: the spy studies the aftermath rather than turning away.
**NPC tier:** 4
**Status:** Active

### 4 — Verdant Circle agent in the Iron Dominion
**NPC name:** Oswin Creel *(cover identity — cartographer)*
**Cover identity:** Military cartographer. Two years in post. Strong performance reviews. Produces accurate survey maps of non-sensitive territory.
**True loyalty:** The Verdant Circle
**Handler:** Speaker Ilen
**Infiltrated faction:** The Iron Dominion (cartography division)
**Location:** Ledger Row — Cartography House (Ironthorn posting)
**Mission:** Monitor Dominion expansion routes; identify which survey operations are approaching Circle-protected forest boundaries; report timeline for intervention.
**Burn condition:** Commander Syla runs a supply audit. The agent's survey maps show protected Circle forest terrain in unusual detail — implying access that no official survey authorisation covers.
**Player detection:** Perception rank 7+ during a Dominion debrief scene. The map detail is visible if the player is paying attention.
**NPC tier:** 4 *(roster entry as Tier 2 Cartographer — cover identity underranked deliberately)*
**Status:** Active

### 5 — Gray Compact agent in the Iron Dominion
**NPC name:** *(unnamed — Ironthorn-external; Dominion posting)*
**Cover identity:** Procurement officer. Four years in post. Strong performance reviews. Clean expense records — almost unusually clean.
**True loyalty:** The Gray Compact
**Handler:** Factor Renne Saul (different handler from the Covenant operation; compartmentalised)
**Infiltrated faction:** The Iron Dominion (supply chain administration)
**Mission:** Monitor contract pricing and resource allocation — specifically tracking what the Dominion is spending on and whether the expansion timeline is accelerating.
**Burn condition:** A Dominion internal investigation into contract pricing anomalies reaches the agent's procurement records. The agent sabotages the investigation, which leaves a trace that Auditor Vesh would catch immediately. If Vesh has already defected, the player is the only one positioned to catch it.
**Player detection:** Perception rank 8+ during any Dominion administrative quest. The sabotage trace is subtle — a requisition order that references an investigation that was officially closed.
**NPC tier:** 4
**Status:** Active — burn risk escalating if Dominion internal investigation continues

### 6 — Void Eternum agent in the Ashen Veil (Quorum Member Issara)
**NPC name:** Issara *(single name; Quorum title: Arch-Necromancer)*
**Cover identity:** Arch-Necromancer; full Quorum member. Seven years inside the Veil. Genuine expertise — her research contributions are real.
**True loyalty:** The Void Eternum (specifically: the Second Unwritten)
**Handler:** The Second Unwritten
**Infiltrated faction:** The Ashen Veil (Pale Quorum — highest tier)
**Mission:** Monitor the threshold experiment. The Eternum wants to know if the Veil's plan to make death optional would, paradoxically, accelerate the conditions for unmaking — a world without death is a world that cannot be sustained, and collapse at that scale interests them.
**Burn condition:** Vorath completes the threshold model and the Quorum votes on whether to proceed. Issara votes yes — which is the correct vote for her cover. But she asks one question before voting that no member committed to the Veil's agenda would ask. Vorath registers it. Does not act immediately.
**Player detection:** Perception rank 9+ during any Quorum-level Veil scene. Issara's question is visible as anomalous only if the player has sufficient Perception to read it against her established pattern.
**NPC tier:** 4
**Status:** Active — most deeply embedded spy in the registry; highest consequence burn

### 7 — Crimson Throne agent in the Aureate Covenant
**NPC name:** Torven Ashcroft
**Cover identity:** Minor noble seeking spiritual absolution for unspecified sins. Has been tithing significantly to the Covenant for eighteen months. Assigned a spiritual director (Brother Aldric).
**True loyalty:** The Crimson Throne
**Handler:** Court Architect Mival (the Throne's intelligence coordinator)
**Infiltrated faction:** The Aureate Covenant (lay aristocratic donor layer)
**Location:** Sanctum Ward — penitent caseload, assigned to Brother Aldric
**Mission:** Map the Covenant's internal political structure; identify which Triune member would be most receptive to a back-channel approach if the deadlock breaks the wrong way for the Throne.
**Burn condition:** Brother Aldric's growing suspicion of the Covenant's internal operations causes him to review his caseload. Torven's tithing pattern looks like payment for access rather than genuine devotion. Aldric has not yet made the connection — but his disillusionment score is rising.
**Player detection:** Perception rank 7+ during a scene with Aldric. The player can read his unease before he can articulate it.
**NPC tier:** 4
**Status:** Active

### 8 — Gray Compact missing agent (Void Eternum profile operation)
**NPC name:** Rynn Ashav
**Cover identity:** Field researcher. Assigned to build a profile of the Void Eternum following intelligence suggesting an organised nihilist network.
**True loyalty:** The Gray Compact
**Handler:** Factor Renne Saul
**Infiltrated faction:** Unknown — tasked with infiltrating the Void Eternum; current status unknown
**Location:** Last known — The Hollows, Ironthorn
**Handler for Ironthorn contact:** Petrov Seld (luxury merchant cover, Wound Market) — Rynn's dead-drop contact
**Mission:** Build a viable profile of the Eternum's membership and operational structure.
**Burn condition:** N/A — agent is missing. Last check-in was four months ago. Dossier is open.
**Player detection:** N/A for the agent. However: at Perception rank 10, the player begins noticing the observation pattern the agent was trying to map — which means they are now walking the same path. This is the entry point for the Void Eternum storyline.
**NPC tier:** 3 (prior to disappearance)
**Status:** Missing — dossier open at the Gray Compact

## Registry integrity note

This document is maintained by the designer. In-world, it does not exist as a single document. The closest equivalent is the combined institutional memory of:
- The Gray Compact's Auditor Vesh (knows 4 of these operations)
- Inquisitor-Commander Leth (knows 2)
- The Void Eternum's Second Unwritten (knows 1 — her own)
- The player, at maximum Perception across all factions, who can piece together 6 of 8 through play

Operations 6 and 8 are undetectable through normal play. They require specific story junctions to surface.
