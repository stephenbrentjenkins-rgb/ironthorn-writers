Submission requirements at a glance
Criterion 1
Tone
The NPC is tired, not broken. No speeches. No monologuing inner states. Suppression, not performance. Every line should sound like something a real person says in that moment.
Criterion 2
Tell System
Three tiers: surface (Perception 0), mid (5–6), deep (7+). All writable as stage direction. Surface tell always active. Deep tell fires on lie-catch regardless of Perception. Consistent across every scene.
Criterion 3
Flag Accuracy
Flags are set by mechanics, not writers. All boolean flags start at false unless mechanically justified. leverage_held and debt_flag_active travel together — never one without the other.
Criterion 4
Voice
Vocabulary constrained by background. Simpler under pressure, not more eloquent. At least one silence written as stage direction. Three test lines written before drafting — none used in the final file.
Criterion 5 · Tier 3+
Decision Map
Five steps: survival check → leverage check → alignment filter → cunning filter → faction filter. In-world response line specific to this character. Reflects true alignment, not public alignment.
Review Window
Timeline
Standard review: 5 business days. Priority commissions (Tier 4+ or story-critical): 3 business days. Resubmissions after return: 3 business days, returned criteria only.
