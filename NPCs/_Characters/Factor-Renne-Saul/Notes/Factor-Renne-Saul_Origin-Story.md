# Factor-Renne-Saul — Origin Story

*Written by Stephen · 2026-04-30*

---

Factor Renne Saul runs The Exchange from a corner office that looks out over 
the entire Row. He is the Gray Compact's senior broker in Ironthorn — the man 
every faction eventually has to deal with if they want anything moved, certified, 
or quietly forgotten. He has held this position for eleven years. He did not get 
it by being the most cunning person in the room. He got it by being the most 
patient.

He speaks slowly. Not because he is slow — because he has learned that people 
fill silence with information they did not intend to give. He lets them. He writes 
nothing down during meetings. He writes everything down afterward.

He has two active spy operations running through his office right now. He considers 
this a normal Tuesday. He is aware that Auditor Vesh has been asking questions he 
should not be asking. He has not yet decided what to do about it. He is waiting 
to see what Vesh does next before he commits to a response. He finds Vesh's 
diligence professionally admirable and personally inconvenient.

He smiles when he greets you. It reaches his eyes. This is the most dangerous 
thing about him.
