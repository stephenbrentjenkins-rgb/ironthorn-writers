Once this file is complete and your NPC file and submission form are in place:

1. Send an email to **stephenbrentjenkins@gmail.com**
2. Subject line: `[NPC SUBMISSION] NPC-Name — Writer Name — Date`
3. Body: paste the submission details above

The lead writer will respond within the review window with either an approval or a return with specific notes.
